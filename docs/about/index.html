About

DMS-MaPseq

Dimethyl sulfate mutational profiling with sequencing (DMS-MaPseq) is a method for determining the base pairing status of adenosine and cytidine nucleotides in an RNA molecule, towards solving its overall secondary or tertiary structure. DMS-MaPseq was invented by Zubradt et al. (2017) and further developed in subsequent works:

  • Tomezsko et al. (2020)

  • Dülk and Rouskin (2022)

  • Bohn et al. (2023)
